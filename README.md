# R-for-Stata-friends
 Informal R Course for Friends Who Know Stata (FWKS)
 
 
## Topics to cover

## Basics

1. [RStudio](https://rstudio.com/)
1. [tidyverse](https://www.tidyverse.org/)
1. [Arranging data with dplyr](https://dplyr.tidyverse.org/)
1. [RMarkdown for making dynamic documents](https://rmarkdown.rstudio.com/)

## Descriptive statistics

1. [stargazer](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/stargazer/vignettes/stargazer.pdf)
1. [tableone](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/tableone/vignettes/introduction.html)
1. [Plotting data with ggplot](https://r4ds.had.co.nz/data-visualisation.html)

 - [lfe package](https://rdrr.io/cran/lfe/man/felm.html)
 - [fixest package](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/fixest/vignettes/fixest_walkthrough.html)
1. [hierarhical models with lmer](http://doingbayesiandataanalysis.blogspot.com/2019/07/shrinkage-in-hierarchical-models-random.html)
1. [broom for collecting coefficients](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/broom/vignettes/broom.html)
